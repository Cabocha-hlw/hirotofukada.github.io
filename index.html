About

I work across industry and academia as a Data Scientist, Product Manager, and Research Assistant. My work spans the design of data products and analytical solutions in practice, as well as research on LLMs, network science, and supply-chain systems. My core strength lies in structuring ambiguous real-world problems into rigorous analytical frameworks, and turning them into actionable strategies, decision-support systems, and research outputs.
